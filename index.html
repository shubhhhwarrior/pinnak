National Tourist Place in India

Explore India's national tourist places and capture the essence of the country's rich history and cultural heritage.

Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Gateway of India

Built in 1911, this gateway stands as a symbol of the migration to India.

Red Fort

Situated in Delhi, this fort was once the seat of power of the Mughal Emperors.

Golden Temple

Built in the 16th century, this temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and attracts millions of devotees annually.
